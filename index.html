Welcome to my portfolio

I am Virendra Rajput, currently working as a Data Analyst at LYCEUM GURUKUL PVT. LTD and formerly a Software Engineer at Kellton Tech Solutions India. Although my academic background is in Mechanical Engineering, my passion for numbers and patterns led me to discover a natural talent for data analysis—and I have pursued it ever since.

I have over a year of industry experience transforming raw information into actionable insights. My toolkit spans Power BI, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Advanced Excel, Python, Web Scraping, and Google Sheets, enabling me to work comfortably with complex datasets and deliver clear stories that drive better business decisions.

I’m also familiar with the Microsoft Power Platform: Power Apps lets me craft intuitive user interfaces, while Power Automate streamlines repetitive workflows—boosting efficiency and reducing the risk of human error.

Beyond the office, I enjoy open‑data challenges. My work was recognised among the top 20 entries in the Transportation & Mobility domain by Codebasics—an achievement that fuels my drive to keep learning and experimenting with new datasets.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I’m excited to connect with recruiters and welcome freelance opportunities that let me solve real‑world problems through data.
